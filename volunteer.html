Cultural Heritage Trust Volunteer Opportunities

Did you want to be an archaeologist as a kid? Are you a student and want to find out what archaeology is all about? Looking for a unique opportunity to volunteer in the community? Join the growing list of dedicated Cultural Heritage Trust volunteers and help the SCDNR Cultural Heritage Trust team preserve our shared cultural heritage.

Volunteers have been an essential part of the SCDNR Cultural Heritage Trust program for over 20 years. Volunteers of all ages and backgrounds help us excavate archaeological site, process artifacts in the laboratory, and even manage hiking trails.

Fill out the form today to add your email to the Cultural Heritage Trust Volunteer mailing list for updates on volunteer opportunities across South Carolina.
